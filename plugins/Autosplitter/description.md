Autosplitter allows you to easily time speedruns of [Don't Look Down](https://gimkit.wiki/wiki/Don%27t_Look_Down), [Fishtopia](https://gimkit.wiki/wiki/Fishtopia), and [One Way Out](https://gimkit.wiki/wiki/One_Way_Out). Don't Look Down can be configured to be either the whole game or a single summit, as well as letting you pick between which physics version you are using.
